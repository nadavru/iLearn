About


Machine Learning is taking a bigger part in the lives of all of us and because of that we think that every one should know how it works.
Useing iLearnML we hope to develop interactive, visualization-based program for teaching selected subjects in ML to nonmajors and highschool students and allow advance users an easy way to visualize the ML models they build.
